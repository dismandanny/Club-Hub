Clubs

17 for Peace and Justice

17 for Peace and Justice exists to leverage the privilege and resources of its UMD student-body membership for the good of communities that are fighting environmental injustices. The organization is guided by the 17 Principles of Environmental Justice established at the First National People of Color Environmental Leadership Summit.

32 Bars

32 Bars is a musical performance troupe dedicated to the performance of musicals, both as entire works and as singular pieces in cabarets. Our goal is to put on full musical productions for UMD students to participate in and attend.

4Corners International Group

4Corners is an International Christian student group on the University of Maryland.
